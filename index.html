Hi, my name is Jamie.

I'm a trainee Software developer with Transport for London and an Anthropology graduate from Durham University.

I believe that the value of technology comes from the people who use it it.

As such, a humanistic approach needs to be applied to what is increasingly becoming an array of complex and impersonal technologies. With this in mind, I want to apply my anthropological background to software development to build products that adopt this approach and have a lasting impact on how we interact with the world.

I love experimenting with new technologies and implementing them into my working environment. I like creating digital and analogue systems to streamline my workflow to achieve maximum productivity.

I'm a motorbike enthusiast, and in my free time I like to cook, play backgammon, and play the piano.
